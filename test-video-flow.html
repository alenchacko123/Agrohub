ðŸ“‹ How the Video System Works

Step 1: Admin Adds a Video

The admin logs into admin-dashboard.html and navigates to "Manage Tutorial Videos". They click "Add New Video" and fill in the form with:

  • Video Title: e.g., "Tractor Operation Basics"
  • Description: What the video teaches
  • Video URL: YouTube URL (any format works - it auto-converts to embed format)
  • Category: Equipment Operation, Maintenance, Safety Guidelines, or Farming Techniques
  • Difficulty Level: Beginner, Intermediate, or Advanced
  • Duration: e.g., "25 min"
  • Instructor Name: e.g., "Ramesh Kumar"
  • Topics: Comma-separated tags

Step 2: Video is Saved to Database

When the admin clicks "Save Video", the video details are stored in the videos table in the MySQL database with status='active'.

Step 3: Farmer Sees the Video

When a farmer logs into farmer-dashboard.html and clicks on the "Skill Center" or "Tutorials" button, they are taken to tutorials.html which:

  • Fetches all active videos from the database via php/get_videos.php
  • Displays them in a beautiful card grid
  • When clicked, opens a modal with the embedded YouTube video

Step 4: Watch Video

The farmer clicks "Start Learning" or clicks on a video card, and a modal opens with the YouTube video playing. All videos added by the admin will appear here automatically!
